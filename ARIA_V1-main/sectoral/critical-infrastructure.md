## Key Obligations

EU AI Act: AI used as safety components in critical infrastructure management systems is an explicit Annex III high-risk category. Includes energy grid management AI, water treatment AI, transport management AI, and digital infrastructure management AI. Full high-risk deployer obligations from August 2026. NIS2 cybersecurity obligations also apply to AI systems in critical infrastructure.

US: NIST CSF 2.0 is the baseline for AI risk in critical infrastructure. Sector-specific agencies (DOE for energy, DOT for transport, EPA for water) may issue AI-specific guidance. CISA has published AI cybersecurity guidelines for critical infrastructure operators.
