## Key Obligations

EU AI Act: Employment and worker management is an explicit Annex III high-risk category. AI used for: recruitment screening, CV filtering, interview assessment, task allocation, performance monitoring, promotion, and termination decisions — all potentially in scope. High-risk deployer obligations apply from August 2026.

US EEOC: Title VII disparate impact analysis applies to AI hiring tools. Four-fifths rule for adverse impact. EEOC has issued guidance and indicated active scrutiny of AI-based screening. NLRB also examining AI in collective bargaining and worker monitoring.

NYC Local Law 144: Annual independent bias audit mandatory for automated employment decision tools used for NYC hiring or promotion. Audit results must be publicly posted. Candidates must be notified before the tool is used. Applies now.

Illinois AI Video Interview Act: Employers using AI to analyse video interviews of Illinois applicants must: notify applicants; explain AI use; obtain consent; limit sharing. In force.
