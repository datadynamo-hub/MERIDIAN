## Structural Baseline

### DPDPA 2023 (enacted, rules pending as of May 2025)
Applies to processing personal data of Indian data principals in digital form. Key obligations: lawful basis for processing; data principal rights (access, correction, erasure, grievance); consent requirements; Data Fiduciary accountability. Rules to specify AI-specific obligations — not yet notified as of May 2025.

### MeitY AI Advisory (March 2024 — partially walked back)
Required platforms to label AI-generated content and obtain government approval before deploying AI deemed "unreliable." Significant industry pushback. Current posture: advisory, not mandatory. Do not present as binding obligation.
