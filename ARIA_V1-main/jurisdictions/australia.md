## Structural Baseline

### Privacy Act 1988 (binding)
Australian Privacy Principles apply to AI processing of personal data. APP 1 (open and transparent management); APP 3 (collection); APP 11 (security). No specific AI provision but OAIC guidance applies existing APPs to AI. Privacy Act reform ongoing — AG has recommended automated decision-making provisions.

### APRA CPS 230 (binding for APRA-regulated entities)
Operational risk management standard. AI models are within scope as operational risk. No AI-specific provision but APRA expects boards to understand and manage AI model risk.

### DISR Voluntary AI Safety Standard (2024)
10 guardrails for responsible AI. Referenced in government procurement. Not mandatory.
