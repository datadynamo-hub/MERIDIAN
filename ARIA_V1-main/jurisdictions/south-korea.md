## Structural Baseline

### PIPA Art.37-2 (in force — 2023 amendments)
Right of data subjects to opt out of automated decisions with significant effect on rights or interests. Right to request human review of such decisions. Right to explanation of basis for automated decision. Applies to controllers processing personal data of Korean data subjects.

### AI Basic Act (pending)
South Korea developing comprehensive AI governance law. Post-AI Seoul Summit (May 2024) political momentum. Likely to create risk-based framework. Timeline uncertain.
