## Structural Baseline

### Three operative AI provisions (all enforced by CAC)
1. Generative AI Measures (2023): Applies to providing generative AI services to the public in China. Obligations: content compliance (socialist values alignment); security assessment before launch; algorithm filing; labelling AI-generated content; user data protection under PIPL.
2. Algorithmic Recommendation Provisions (2022): Applies to recommendation algorithm services with Chinese users. Obligations: transparency to users; opt-out mechanism; no use of algorithms to target vulnerable users; labelling.
3. Deep Synthesis Provisions (2022): Applies to deepfake/synthetic media. Obligations: watermarking; consent for voice/likeness; verification of user identity.

### Expected comprehensive AI law
China Basic AI Law — in development. Expected to create horizontal framework similar in scope to EU AI Act. Timeline uncertain — draft not published as of May 2025.
