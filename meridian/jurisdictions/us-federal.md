## Structural Baseline

### Expected operative frameworks
- NIST AI RMF 1.0 (2023) — voluntary, de facto enterprise baseline. NIST AI 600-1 for GPAI.
- SR 11-7 (OCC/Fed) — model risk management for banking institutions. Pre-AI but applies.
- CFPB: Adverse action notice requirements apply to AI-based credit decisions (ECOA/Reg B).
- EEOC: Title VII disparate impact applies to AI screening tools; EEOC issued employer guidance.
- FDA: AI/ML SaMD guidance for healthcare AI qualifying as medical device software.

### Expected federal AI policy posture (post Jan 2025)
Pro-innovation, reduced precautionary requirements. NIST AI Safety Institute restructured — research function retained, safety-first mandate modified. Expect reduced federal AI governance appetite; increased state-level variation as a result.
