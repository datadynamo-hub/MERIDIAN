## Structural Baseline

### PDPA (binding)
Applies to processing personal data of Singapore individuals. No AI-specific provisions but general data protection obligations apply. PDPC has issued advisory guidelines on AI — non-binding but reflect PDPC's enforcement posture.

### MAS FEAT Principles (binding for MAS-regulated entities via supervisory expectations)
Fairness, Ethics, Accountability, Transparency. MAS-regulated firms using AI in financial services expected to align. Not statute but carries enforcement weight — MAS can require remediation.

### IMDA Model AI Governance Framework (voluntary)
Two editions (2019, 2020). Widely referenced in Singapore government procurement. AI Verify testing toolkit available for voluntary self-assessment.
