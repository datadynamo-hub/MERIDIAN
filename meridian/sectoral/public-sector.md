## Key Obligations

EU AI Act: Public authorities face stricter obligations than private deployers for several categories. Real-time biometric identification in public spaces prohibited except narrow law enforcement exemption. Social scoring by public authorities prohibited outright. Public authorities deploying high-risk AI in essential services must register in EU database.

UK: Algorithmic Transparency Recording Standard — public bodies using algorithmic tools in significant decisions affecting people must publish a record. Still being rolled out. AI Procurement guidance for public sector.

US Federal: OMB M-24-10 requires federal agencies to: designate Chief AI Officers; conduct annual AI use case inventories; implement AI governance policies; apply rights-impacting AI safeguards. Check current status post-January 2025 administration change.
