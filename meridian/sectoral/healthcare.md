## Key Obligations

EU AI Act: AI systems used as safety components in medical devices covered under MDR/IVDR may fall under both regimes. High-risk classification applies to AI diagnostic and clinical decision support tools meeting Annex III criteria. Conformity assessment required.

US FDA: AI/ML-enabled SaMD subject to 510(k), De Novo, or PMA pathway depending on risk. Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP) framework for iteratively improving AI. Not a new concept but implementation guidance developing.

UK MHRA: Broadly aligned to FDA approach via UK MDR. Software and AI as Medical Device Change Programme ongoing — monitor for updated guidance.
